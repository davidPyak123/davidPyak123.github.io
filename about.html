About Nepali Culture

This page is about Nepali culture and traditions. Nepal is a sovereign state location in the south Asian. The most beautiful, landlock and diverse country between India and China. Nepal is the only official Hindu country in the world with more than 90 percent of its population following the Hindu religion. As well as, Buddha was bone in Lumbini, a zone of Nepal. The 4.20 percent of population adore Buddhism.

The propose of this page is to provide information about the rich and unique mother-tongue and culture of Nepal. Nepali people spoke more than 20 languages', which is included Nepali, Maithili, Bhojpuri, Tharu, Tamang, Limbu, Gurung, and Sharpe. Those languages are most popular in among an area.
